**🏆 Best Paper Award**

This paper proposes a technique to reduce redundant interpolation computations in fractional motion estimation for HEVC (H.265) video compression. By identifying and eliminating repeated interpolation operations across prediction unit boundaries, the method reduces computational cost while maintaining coding efficiency.
